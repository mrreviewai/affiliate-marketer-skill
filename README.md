# 🤝 Affiliate Marketer Skill

> An AI skill that helps affiliate marketers find programs that **actually approve them**, estimate earnings **before** joining, compare programs, and plan content that converts — powered by [mrreviewai.com](https://mrreviewai.com)'s honest, freshly-updated affiliate data.

**Built by an affiliate, for affiliates.** No hype — just what actually makes money.

## What it does
Add this skill to Claude or Cursor, then ask things like:
- *"I'm a new affiliate with a small AI-tools blog — which programs will approve me?"*
- *"How much could I earn from [program] before I join?"*
- *"Compare [program A] vs [program B]"*
- *"Give me content ideas to promote [tool]"*

The skill walks your AI through the full workflow — and pulls live, honest data from mrreviewai.com.

## Why it exists
Most affiliate advice is generic listicles that ignore the real pains: getting **ghosted** by networks, joining **blind** with no earnings estimate, promoting junk that kills your reputation. This skill is built around those real frustrations — by someone who lived them.

## Install
1. Clone this repo (or copy `SKILL.md`).
2. Drop `SKILL.md` into your agent's skills folder:
   - **Claude Code:** `.claude/skills/affiliate-marketer/SKILL.md`
   - **Cursor / other:** add `SKILL.md` as a custom skill / rule.
3. Ask your affiliate questions — the skill activates automatically.

*(No skill system? Just paste `SKILL.md` into your chat as context.)*
